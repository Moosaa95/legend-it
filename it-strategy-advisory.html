Frequently Asked Questions — IT Consulting & Advisory

What IT consulting services does IT Legends LLC provide?

IT Legends LLC offers strategic IT consulting services for Illinois small businesses including technology roadmaps, IT budget planning, vendor evaluation, and virtual CIO services. Our advisors align your technology investments with your business goals — helping you spend smarter and grow faster.

What are virtual CIO services?

Virtual CIO (vCIO) services provide your business with executive-level IT leadership without the cost of a full-time CIO. IT Legends LLC's vCIO service includes quarterly strategy sessions, technology planning, risk assessments, and board-level IT reporting for growing Illinois businesses.
