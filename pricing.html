How Pricing Works

Base Bundle + Add-Ons

Each bundle includes a base number of users or devices. As your team grows, we adjust pricing in a predictable way.

  • Base monthly fee per bundle
  • Per-user or per-device add-ons above the included amount
  • Microsoft 365 licensing billed per user via Pax8
  • Optional add-ons for advanced security & backup

Flexible for Small & Growing Teams

We understand many clients are cost-conscious. That’s why we focus on:

  • Right-sizing your tools — no unnecessary extras
  • Security-first configuration without enterprise overhead
  • Clear communication and no surprise invoices

What’s Not Included by Default

To keep your pricing fair and predictable, some items are handled as separate projects or add-ons.

Project-Based Work

  • Major migrations or rebuilds
  • Large-scale hardware refreshes
  • New office builds or moves

After-Hours & Emergency Support

  • Support outside normal business hours
  • Emergency response for critical outages
  • After-hours work may include a surcharge
